# GEO Core Concepts

## GEO

Generative Engine Optimization. The goal is to improve a source's visibility, citation, and factual influence inside AI-generated answers.

## AEO

Answer Engine Optimization. Often used for answer boxes and direct-answer surfaces. GEO overlaps with AEO but focuses more on generative engines and AI search.

## Prompt Universe

A structured set of real user questions across roles, intents, constraints, languages, and follow-ups.

## Evidence Atom

A small claim-evidence unit:

- claim,
- source,
- time range,
- applicable scope,
- limitation,
- reusable answer sentence,
- review status.

## Citation Selection

The stage where an AI search platform chooses a source as a citation or supporting document.

## Citation Absorption

The stage where information from a cited source actually shapes the generated answer.

## Authority Network

The set of self-owned and external sources that support the same brand/product facts: official site, docs, media, social, reviews, directories, communities, papers, videos, and partner pages.

## AIVO Score

A report-style composite score used in the sample diagnosis output. In this project, treat it as a product/report framing, not a universal industry standard.
